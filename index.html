Natural numbers are whole numbers from 1 upwards : 1, 2, 3, and so on ... or from 0 upwards in some area of mathematics: 0, 1, 2, 3 and so on ... No negative numbers and no fractions. Test Data: console.log(is_Natural(-15)); console.log(is_Natural(1)); console.log(is_Natural(10.22)); console.log(is_Natural(10/0));
